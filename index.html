About SLYE

The Sierra Leone Youth Empowerment Initiative (SLYE) is a five-year national program designed to empower Adolescent Girls, Young Women, and Adolescent Boys across all districts of Sierra Leone.

Our Objectives

  • Provide life skills & comprehensive sexuality education to 1 million youth.
  • Increase economic opportunities for 500,000 young people.
  • Establish 10,000 Youth Hubs nationwide.
  • Promote gender equality and youth-friendly policies.

Key Programs

  • Life Skills & Financial Literacy Training
  • Vocational & Digital Skills Development
  • Youth Entrepreneurship Support
  • Gender-Based Violence Advocacy
  • Youth Policy Engagement & Leadership
